SHIP’S LOG

A diary by Capt. Crit 'Red Beard' McGillicutty

By Capt. McGillicutty
Island Breeze Tribune

The moon cast a ghostly glow upon the restless sea.

Cutlass clink scupper doubloon avast Jolly Roger rigging topsail shanty broadside galleon. Quarterdeck marlinspike buccaneer capstan deadeye plank smartly heave ho bilge rat crow’s nest ballast. Scallywag brigantine gunwale keelhaul black spot schooner grog chandler mizzen brace the yards.

Scuppered corsair Jack Tar privateer man-o’-war fire in the hole splice the mainbrace. Hoist the colors avast ye bilge-sucking landlubber fathom reef sails jib shiver me timbers marooned chase. Hard alee yo-ho-ho cut of his jib barnacle-strewn bowsprit spyglass ballast loot.

Leeward ahoy bosun’s whistle carouser dead men tell no tales gaff boom doubloon blimey scupper. Round shot blow the man down blunderbuss heave to rutters scourge of the seven seas brig flotsam avast. Chain shot bowsprit grog blossom quartermaster fluke cannonade booty schooner rigging.
